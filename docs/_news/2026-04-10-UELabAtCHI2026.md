The User Empowerment Lab is heading to Barcelona for the ACM CHI 2026 conference. Daisy Chen will be presenting new work on Engagement-Prolonging Designs and Chris Fu will be presenting new work on using AI in the design process. Lab members are also contributors on several other papers. Past and current lab members will be attending the <href a="https://chi2026.darkpatternsresearchandimpact.com/">Bridge Over Troubled Water</href> workshop on dark patterns.
