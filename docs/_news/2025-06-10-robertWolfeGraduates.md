The UE Lab congratulates Robert Wolfe, who completed his PhD in Information Science this spring! His dissertation examines the epistemic risks of generative AI and potential mitigations. He will be joining the the School of Communication and Information at Rutgers University as an assistant professor in the fall of 2025. Congratulations, Robert; we will miss you!
