Emmi is a PhD candidate in the Information School and is a member of the <a href=”https://www.userempowerment.org/”>User Empowerment Lab</a>, <a href=”https://gamer.ischool.uw.edu/”>GAMER Group</a>, and <a href=”https://digitalyouth.uw.edu/”>Center for Digital Youth</a>. She studies how the design of digital technology presents both risks and opportunities for children’s autonomy. She is particularly passionate about studying young children’s digital gaming practices through playful, child-led design and research methods. Prior to pursuing her PhD, Emmi was a professional full-stack software developer at Microsoft and Smartsheet.
