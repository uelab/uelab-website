Rotem Landesman explores young people’s ethical thinking about emerging technologies, and particularly, AI. She is a PhD candidate at the Information School at the University of Washington, co-advised by Katie Davis and Amy J. Ko. Her work spans the computing education and human-computer interaction fields. She is part of the Center for Digital Youth and works closely with a variety of interdisciplinary collaborators. She is also a Graduate Student Researcher at foundry10.
