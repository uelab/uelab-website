Erin Beneteau is Director of Instructional Design and Responsible AI at Microsoft. She leads education initiatives on responsible AI policies and practices to make sure that AI systems are trustworthy by design. She partners with engineering, corporate functions, legal, public policy, and field teams to develop curriculum and learning experiences. She is also a researcher specializing in human computer interactions (HCI), and utilizes her background as a communication specialist to analyze natural language interactions with conversational agents.
