Chris' research focuses on Human-AI teaming. In particular, he studies Human-AI co-creation, AI memory and personalization, and long-context, multi-turn communication between humans and AI. He examines these phenomena in domains like AI-mediated communication, AI-supported design, music production, learning, and sense-making. He also designs and implements conversational agents.
